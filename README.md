## 💡 What is BeyWallet?

BeyWallet lets you send and receive Bitcoin ecash easily and securely on your Windows computer. It uses local storage, so your data stays on your device. It supports Cashu, Nostr, and Bitcoin, bringing privacy and hardware-level security to your digital wallet. Designed for users without tech skills, BeyWallet keeps your money safe without complicated setup.

## 🔐 Setting Up Your Wallet

After installation, launch BeyWallet from the Start Menu or your desktop shortcut.

### Creating a New Wallet

1. Click **Create New Wallet**.
2. You will see a seed phrase (a list of words). Write it down carefully and keep it safe. This phrase is the only way to recover your wallet.
3. Confirm the seed phrase by entering the words as requested.
4. Set a strong password to protect your wallet on this computer.

### Importing an Existing Wallet

If you have a seed phrase from another Cashu or Bitcoin wallet:

1. Click **Import Wallet**.
2. Enter your seed phrase exactly.
3. Choose a new password.

## 💵 Making Payments with BeyWallet

BeyWallet works with Bitcoin ecash and Cashu tokens. Sending and receiving payments is simple.

### Sending Bitcoin ecash

1. Open the wallet app.
2. Click **Send**.
3. Enter the recipient’s address or scan their Nostr QR code.
4. Type in the amount you want to send.
5. Confirm the payment.
6. The wallet will process the transaction securely.

### Receiving Bitcoin ecash

1. Go to the **Receive** tab.
2. Share your address or QR code with the payer.
3. Watch for confirmation of incoming funds.

### Using Hardware Wallets

If you have a hardware wallet (like Ledger or Trezor), you can connect it via USB:

1. Go to **Settings > Hardware Wallet**.
2. Select your device and follow the on-screen prompts.
3. Operations will require you to approve on the hardware device.
